Sewing Work

Sewing is the craft or skill of joining fabrics together using stitches made with a needle and thread. It's one of the oldest textile arts, dating back thousands of years. Sewing can be done by hand with a needle and thread or by using a sewing machine.

Tailor

A tailor is a skilled craftsman who specializes in creating and altering garments to fit individual body shapes and preferences. With meticulous attention to detail, tailors use a combination of measurements, patterns, and sewing techniques to produce well-fitted and stylish clothing
